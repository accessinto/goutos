The Peloponnese is a national area that is located on a South European Greek peninsula making up the most of the south part of the Greece mainland.

The Peloponnese is considered as "true Greece" combining many centuries of ancient history and mythology. The region is split up into several sub-regions. The North-West area of The Peloponnese is rich in history and great as holiday destination.

The Peloponnese also includes "Olympia" an ancient town and archaeological site located in the west part of the Peloponnese. One of the most ancient Greek sanctuaries is called the "Valley of Gods" (where the Olympic Games originated from). Olympia is a great place to visit when you are visiting the Peloponnese.

Start a new experience and visit the old ruins in Greece. Are you interested in spending a great time embracing the Greek sun and having fun with watersports (snorkeling, waterskiing, sailing) then a holiday in the Peloponnese is just great for you and your family. Greece will always be an attractive holiday destination.

As a holiday destination the Peloponnese has a lot to offer such as pristine beaches, cosy localities and ancient archaeological sites. The Hellenic peninsula continues ro attract many tourists from all over the planet.
